### Key Verification Points
- ISO 10015:2019 is a **guidelines document**, NOT a management system standard
- Does NOT use ISO High Level Structure (HLS/Annex SL) — no Clause 4 "Context", no Clause 6 "Planning", no Clause 10 "Improvement"
- Substantive content is in **Clause 4** (Competence management) and **Clause 5** (Competence management and people development) only
