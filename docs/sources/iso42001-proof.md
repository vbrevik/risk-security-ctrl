## Verification Results

### Confirmed Correct
- **Framework metadata:** Name, version (2023), source_url all correct
- **Clauses 4-10:** All mandatory management system clauses present with correct names following Harmonized Structure
- **Clause 8 AI-specific additions:** AI risk assessment (8.2), AI risk treatment (8.3), AI system impact assessment (8.4) correctly included
- **Annex A domains (A.2-A.10):** All 9 domains present and correctly named
- **Annex A numbering:** Correctly starts at A.2 (no A.1)
- **Annex B:** Implementation guidance present with reasonable domain structure

### Issues Found
1. **Annex A control count:** File has 24 sub-controls but standard has 38. Approximately 14 individual controls are missing across the domains. Requires purchased standard to identify exactly which.
2. **Annex B sub-control accuracy:** Specific sub-control titles (B.1.1-B.4.4) cannot be independently verified from public sources. May be paraphrased.
3. **Annexes C and D missing:** Standard includes Annex C (AI-related organizational objectives and risk sources) and Annex D (Use of AIMS across domains/sectors) — not represented.

83 concepts — all structurally legitimate. Missing ~14 Annex A controls that exist in the standard.
